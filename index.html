What is EPE Membership?

A free membership for every Wichitan — and the organizations behind them.

EPE Membership is a free, community-powered membership connecting Wichita residents to opportunities, events, resources, and local impact. Individuals join to find their next step; organizations join as partners to reach and serve members. No cost, no application — just sign up.
